Ann Williams founded her Hong Kong-based writing and editing company, Write On, in 1990.

It has built up a strong track record in editorial and corporate fields.
Strengths include a solid background gained in major news-gathering organisations and experience garnered in working on prestigious and well regarded magazines, both editorial and in the area of contract publishing for major Hong Kong and international corporations, educational institutions and quasi-government organisations.
