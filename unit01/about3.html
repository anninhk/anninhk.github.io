About Me

I'm a freelance writer and editor; I live in Hong Kong, though I am originally from the UK. I became interested in Front-End Web Design and Development because I am increasingly being asked by clients to provide website content, and I want to learn more about the process and structure of websites, just in the way that I understand the mechanics behind newspaper and magazine design and printing.

My Mission

As I learn more about front-end, I hope to be able to increase the proportion of work on websites and to promote myself as an experienced provider of website content. In the future, I would consider designing websites for small local companies in a range of fields including e-retailing, health and fitness, art and design, etc.
